--- layout: post title: VMWare vmmod.tar missing during installation date: '2007-09-19T04:02:00.000-07:00' author: Gert Lombard tags: modified_time: '2007-09-19T04:10:21.580-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1583243090910089198.post-4276882588992456312 blogger_orig_url: http://www.lombard.me/2007/09/vmware-vmmodtar-missing-during.html --- Yesterday I had to reinstall VMWare Workstation 6.0 on my Ubuntu 7.04 PC, and again I got stuck during the installation with the error about vmmod.tar not being found under /usr/lib/vmware/modules/source/

The solution is simple and widely publicized but still many people seem to sit with this problem.

Just create the /usr/lib/vmware/modules/source/ directory if necessary, then get the vmware-any-any patch from http://knihovny.cvut.cz/ftp/pub/vmware/ (e.g. vmware-any-any-update113.tar.gz) and run the runme.pl to continue the installation.
